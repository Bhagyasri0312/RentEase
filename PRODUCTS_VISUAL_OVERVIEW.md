## 📝 **How to Use**

### **Search for Products**

1. Click the search bar at the top
2. Type a product name or feature
3. Results update in real-time

### **Filter Products**

**Desktop:**

- Use left sidebar filters
- Select any combination
- Results update instantly

**Mobile:**

- Click "Filters" button
- Overlay modal appears
- Select filters
- Click "Apply Filters"

### **Sort Products**

1. Click "Sort By" dropdown
2. Choose sorting method
3. Products reorder immediately

### **Add to Wishlist**

1. Click heart icon on product
2. Heart fills with red
3. Click again to remove

### **Clear Filters**

- Click "Clear All" button
- All filters reset instantly
- Search clears too
